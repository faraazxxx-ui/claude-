## GitHub Gem Seeker

### Shopify Store Setup Automation

No suitable open-source tools were found in this category. While some repositories for Shopify automation exist, they are either not well-maintained, have very few stars, or are too specific to a particular use case.

### E-commerce Inventory Management

No suitable open-source tools were found. Most inventory management systems are either part of a larger e-commerce platform or are proprietary.

### Shipping Label Automation / Drop Shipping

*   **Name:** Karrio
*   **GitHub URL:** https://github.com/karrioapi/karrio
*   **Stars:** 706
*   **Last Commit:** 2026-04-02
*   **Description:** Karrio is an open-source multi-carrier shipping API that simplifies label generation, package tracking, and carrier connections management.
*   **Application to Ghusoon:** Karrio can be self-hosted to automate the entire shipping process for Ghusoon. It can connect to various carriers, get live rates, purchase shipping labels, and provide real-time tracking to customers. This would eliminate the manual shipping process Mrs. Haq is currently using.

*   **Stars:** 4521
*   **Last Commit:** 2026-04-03

### Social Media Marketing Automation (Instagram/Facebook)

No suitable open-source tools were found. The landscape for social media automation is dominated by proprietary services, and open-source tools in this area are often outdated or violate the terms of service of the social media platforms.

### Product Photography Enhancement Tools

No suitable open-source tools were found. While there are many AI-powered image enhancement tools, they are mostly available as paid services. Open-source projects in this area are often research-focused and not production-ready.

## Internet Skill Finder

No pre-existing Agent Skills were found that directly address the needs of the Ghusoon project. The search for skills related to e-commerce, Shopify, business planning, marketing, social media, and accounting did not yield any relevant results.

Based on the project's requirements, the following custom skills should be created:

*   **`shopify-store-manager`**: A skill to manage a Shopify store, including adding products, managing inventory, and processing orders.
*   **`business-plan-generator`**: A skill to generate a business plan based on user input, similar to the BusinessGPT tool found.
*   **`social-media-marketer`**: A skill to automate social media posts and marketing campaigns on platforms like Instagram and Facebook.
*   **`bookkeeper`**: A skill to manage business finances, track income and expenses, and generate financial reports, potentially integrating with a tool like Frappe Books.
