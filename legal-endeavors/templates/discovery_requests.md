# Discovery Request Templates: Rahman v. UHS et al.
## Case No. 3:26-CV-00197 (AJB/ML)

These are pre-drafted discovery requests organized by defendant and priority. Customize and finalize with counsel before serving.

## Interrogatories to UHS

1. Identify all persons who participated in any decision to place Plaintiff on academic probation, including the date of each decision and the basis cited.

2. Identify all complaints, whether formal or informal, anonymous or attributed, made against Plaintiff during his residency (June 2021 – June 2024), including the date, substance, and identity of the complainant if known.

3. Describe in detail the investigation, if any, conducted by UHS into the allegations made by Dr. Sundas Rashid against Plaintiff, including the identity of all persons involved and all documents generated.

4. Identify all communications between any UHS employee and any law enforcement agency regarding Plaintiff, including the date, participants, and substance of each communication.

5. State whether UHS provided any information regarding Plaintiff to any prospective employer, licensing board, or credentialing body after June 22, 2024, and if so, identify the recipient and substance of each communication.

6. Describe all accommodations, if any, provided to Plaintiff following his cardiac surgery in February 2024, and identify the person(s) responsible for accommodation decisions.

7. Identify all residents in the Internal Medicine program from June 2021 through June 2024 who were placed on probation, and for each, state the basis, duration, and whether the resident signed the probation document.
