## 3. Phase 3: "The Lever" (Settlement Accelerant)

**Leverage Points:**
1. **Personal Depositions**: Demand depositions of Ali (Program Director) and DIO (Designated Institutional Official). Use FMLA email as Exhibit A to prove they cannot claim ignorance.
2. **Audio Evidence**: Deploy admission recording ("We knew it is false. We framed him") in settlement negotiations. This is devastating and defendants will want to avoid its use at trial.
3. **Medical Malpractice Overlay**: PICC line coercion as damage multiplier — forcing a septic immunocompromised patient to work transforms employment dispute into reckless endangerment.
4. **Division of Guilt**: Naming individuals forces UHS to choose between defending them (owning misconduct) or abandoning them (validating claims).
5. **Media/Reputation Threat**: The facts of this case are compelling for media coverage — acquitted physician, cardiac patient forced to work, audio admission of framing.
