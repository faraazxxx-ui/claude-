## 8. Key Legal Theories

1. **Cat's Paw Liability**: UHS adopted Sundas's personal vendetta as institutional policy without independent investigation
2. **Apex Doctrine Defeat**: FMLA email proves Ali had unique personal knowledge, defeating claims of ignorance
3. **Business Judgment Piercing**: Forcing a septic resident to work violates medical standards of care, not just employment law
4. **Division of Guilt**: Naming individuals forces UHS to choose between defending them (owning misconduct) or abandoning them (validating claims)
5. **Medical Malpractice Overlay**: PICC line coercion transforms employment dispute into reckless endangerment
